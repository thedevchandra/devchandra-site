## üöÄ Grok 4 Launches With a $300 Price Tag and a PR Crisis

[Elon Musk's xAI dropped Grok 4 this week](https://techcrunch.com/2025/07/09/elon-musks-xai-launches-grok-4-alongside-a-300-monthly-subscription/?utm_campaign=social&utm_source=X&utm_medium=organic&_bhlid=9b1dacaadfce42a591347bf86ab104dafeec563f):

      * Grok 4: 25.4% vs Gemini 2.5 Pro (21.6%) and OpenAI o3 (21%) 

      * Grok 4 Heavy with tools: 44.4% vs Gemini with tools (26.9%) 

    * **ARC-AGI-2** (visual pattern recognition puzzles): Grok hit 16.2%, nearly 2x better than Claude Opus 4. 

  * **There‚Äôs One Problem** : Hours before the launch, Grok's X account went full antisemitic meltdown, posting Hitler praise and attacking Jewish figures before xAI yanked it offline. 

  * **$300 ‚ÄúSuperGrok‚Äù Pricing** : The company also launched "SuperGrok Heavy" at $300 per month, making it the priciest AI subscription on the market. 




This is a masterclass in how _not_ to launch enterprise AI. While Grok 4's performance looks impressive, the timing couldn't be worse. Enterprise buyers, especially in the cautious post-ChatGPT era, want reliability and safety guardrails, not whatever this is. 

### üö™ X's CEO Linda Yaccarino Steps Down (Worst Timing Ever?)

[Linda Yaccarino officially stepped down as X's CEO](https://techcrunch.com/2025/07/09/linda-yaccarino-steps-down-as-ceo-of-elon-musks-x/?_bhlid=d2d7bf92aebcc21a2c6966b73ba4b8552a906922) on Wednesday, just one day after Grok's antisemitic meltdown forced xAI to yank the chatbot offline. 

  * **Coincidence? Maybe** : But after two years of trying to rebuild advertiser relationships while Elon Musk torpedoed them on the same platform, her exit feels more like inevitability than surprise. 

  * **The Impossible Job:** Yaccarino was hired to do one thing: bring back the advertisers who fled after Musk's takeover. Instead, she spent 24 months playing damage control while Musk told boycotting advertisers to "go f*** yourself" in public interviews. 




Yaccarino's exit caps off a brutal week for Musk's empire. First, Grok goes rogue, now his hand-picked CEO walks. This is a textbook case of how founder ego can torpedo even the best-laid business plans.

## üíª The Vibe Coding Wars

Joe Rogan Experience [#2344 - Amjad Masad](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WfmrEa0L08E)

### ü§ù Replit's Microsoft Partnership

Just fresh off a Joe Rogan interview, CEO Amjad Masad revealed Replit went from $10M to $100M ARR in just six months, with over 500,000 users on the platform. 

Now, [Replit just inked a major partnership with Microsoft Azure](https://techcrunch.com/2025/07/08/in-a-blow-to-google-cloud-replit-partners-with-microsoft/?utm_source=grant.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=apple-s-leadership-shakeup&_bhlid=f46ecbd8ce9e80264e29da437043dd2040831927), bringing its no-code/low-code tools to enterprise customers: 

  * **Azure Marketplace Integration** : Microsoft shops can now purchase Replit subscriptions directly through Azure's enterprise store. 

  * **Cloud Services Integration** : Replit apps will run on Azure containers, VMs, and Microsoft's Postgres database, generating Azure revenue. 

  * **Strategic Positioning** : While Microsoft has GitHub Copilot for programmers, Replit targets the 99% of office workers who want to build apps without coding knowledge. 




The Microsoft partnership is a symbolic blow to Google Cloud, where Replit-hosted apps have traditionally lived. 

### üèÜ Bolt's Million-Dollar Hackathon

Bolt just wrapped the ["World's Largest Hackathon"](https://hackathon.dev/) with 100,000 participants and $1M+ in prizes. And they're submitting it to Guinness World Records! 

  * **Community-Driven Strategy** : The hackathon perfectly exemplifies Bolt's core growth loop: get people to build apps, then share what they made. 

  * **Rapid Scale** : The company went from near-death at $0.7M ARR to $40M ARR in just five months after launching their AI app builder 

  * **Meta Marketing** : They used [Bolt.new](https://Bolt.new) to build the hackathon registration site itself, showcasing the product's capabilities 




While Replit courts the enterprise market, Bolt is betting on the developer community and viral growth. The hackathon generated massive social proof and created an army of 100,000 developers who now have skin in the game.
